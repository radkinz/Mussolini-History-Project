A Timeline of Benito Mussolini's Life

1883

Mussolini is born in Predappio, Italy

1902

Mussolini moves to Switzerland

1909

Mussolini moves to Austria-Hungary

1912

Mussolini returns to Italy and becomes editor of the newspaper Avanti!

1919

Mussolini founded Italy's fascist movement

1922

Mussolini leads the March on Rome

1925

Mussolini asserts himself as dictator

1929

Mussolini signs the Lateran Pact

1935

Italy invades Abyssiania

1936

Mussolini sends aid to the General Franco of Spain

1939

Mussolini signs the Pact of Steel

1940

Italy declares war on Britain and France

1945

Mussolini is captured and executed at Lake Como
